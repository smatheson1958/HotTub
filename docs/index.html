Plan your caravan load with more confidence

LoadMate is a simple caravan and Motorhome loading and weight planning app designed to help owners think clearly about payload, loading positions, nose weight and overall caravan and Motorhome balancing.

The app is intended as a practical planning aid. It does not replace manufacturer limits, legal requirements, weighbridge measurements or professional towing advice.

What LoadMate helps with

  • Record caravan/Motorhome loading information.
  • Plan where items are positioned.
  • Review estimated loading impact before travelling.
  • Keep key caravan weight information in one place.

Privacy-first design

LoadMate does not require an account and is designed for local use on your device.
