Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

LoadMate is provided by Curley Brackets Engineering. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Data collection

LoadMate does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No personal information is sent to Curley Brackets Engineering.
  • No advertising tracking is included.
  • No cloud synchronisation service is provided by the app.

Local storage

Information entered into the app is intended to be stored locally on the user's device. Users are responsible for managing their own device backups and data retention.
